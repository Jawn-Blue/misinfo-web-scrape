<h2 class="headlineTitle"><a href="news/14563-texas-gop-rep-biden-is-complicit-in-human-trafficking" title="Texas GOP Rep: Biden Is 'Complicit' in Human Trafficking">Texas GOP Rep: Biden Is 'Complicit' in Human Trafficking</a></h2>
<div class="headlineText"><a href="news/14563-texas-gop-rep-biden-is-complicit-in-human-trafficking" title="Texas GOP Rep: Biden Is 'Complicit' in Human Trafficking">A Texas Republican congressman has accused Joe Biden of being "complicit" in human trafficking across the U.S.-Mexico border.
Rep. Troy Nehls (R-TX) blasted the federal government for allowing migrant children to be released into the interior of the United States.
Nehls says the Biden administration is "aiding and abetting in the crime of human trafficking" by pushing anti-Trump border policy.
The GOP lawmaker slammed Biden over the spiraling crisis at the border during a Monday appearance on "Fox News Primetime."
He told host Maria Bartiromo that the handling of migrant children at the border is "inhumane."
"You're going to see migrant children being warehoused in camps that are full to the gills right now, and the problem is going to continue to get worse as the days and the weeks and the months progress," Rep. Nehls said.</a></div>
