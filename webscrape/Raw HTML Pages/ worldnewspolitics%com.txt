<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2021/02/22/chinese-diplomat-seeks-renewal-of-us-china-diplomatic-relationship-between/" rel="bookmark">Chinese Diplomat Seeks Renewal of US-China Diplomatic Relationship Between</a></h2> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p style="text-align: right;">In a forum held last Feb. 22, senior Chinese diplomat Wang Yi put forward a request for Pres. Biden to restore the good relationship between the US and China. As the <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/06/23/what-are-the-political-factors-that-can-affect-businesses/">diplomatic tie between the two countries was damaged under Trump’s administration</a></strong>, Wang is now urging the U.S. President remove the tariffs on Chinese goods.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/E1US5N6L4Uw?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="China calls for reset in China-US relations" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>Wang’s request included an appeal for the U.S. government to not meddle with Beijing’s handling of its internal affairs, including the country’s handling of the actions launched separatists seeking for the independence of Taiwan.</p>
<p>As the U.S. under the Trump regime had the US cut off any dialogue with Chinese government officials, Wang stated that they are ready to communicate in solving problems that the two countries part in the past few years, Wang believed with optimism that the recent call between US President Biden and Chinese President Xi Jinping is a great and positive indication that both leaders are now ready to take a step towards good relationship.</p>
<h3><strong>Speculations that Pres. Biden Plans to Continue the Fight Vs. China’s Trade Practices</strong></h3>
<p>However, it seems President Biden plans to keep the pressure on Beijing after expressing concerns about <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2019/04/05/chinas-diplomacy-tactics-debt-trap-diplomacy/">China’s unfair and coercive trade practices.</a></strong></p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/-bZCXaoZ5Qs?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="Biden expected to maintain tough stance on China" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>Moreover, other concerns raised by President Biden include China’s continuing claims over territorial rights in the West Philippine Sea, by intimidating neighboring countries. Another important factor that can affect Biden”s cooperation are the allegations of human rights crimes against Uighur Muslim minorities in Xinjiang.</p>

<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/11/24/biden-to-rejoin-paris-climate-accord-once-hes-seated/" rel="bookmark">Biden to Rejoin Paris Climate Accord Once He’s Seated</a></h2> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p>Once President-elect Biden sits in the presidential chair next year, the U.S. will re-enter the Paris Climate Agreement from which Trump backed out in 2017. The agreement was created in 2015, to which the U.S. under the Obama administration, was one of more than 200 countries who made a commitment to take actions in circumventing the terrible effects of climate change predicted by scientists.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/zJ32rM2LBiU?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="Biden vows to rejoin Paris Climate Agreement on day one" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>President-elect Joe Biden gave a promise that he will bring the country back into the folds of the climate accord any moment in February. All that the incoming Biden administration needs to do is to mail a letter to the United Nations as a way of affirming the country’s intent to join the accord again. Once sent, the formal return of the country will be recognized on the following month.</p>
<p>Immediately after the country’s return to the Paris Climate Accord, the U.S. will agree with other member countries in setting down voluntary goals aimed at lowering domestic emissions; and in implementing sterner objectives in related future goals. Additionally, part of each member country’s binding commitment is to submit accurate reports about the progress made in achieving their respective climate change commitments.</p>
<h3><strong>Why Rejoining the Paris Climate Accord is an Important Step for the Biden Administration</strong></h3>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/1xu7TBZOj2o?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="It’s Not Too Late: The Paris climate agreement" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>The next climate talks of the United Nation will be held in Glasgow, Scotland next year in November. Countries are anticipated to present fresh and ambitious goals for 2030, while the focus is expected to center mainly on the goals of thd U.S.</p>
<p>According to the 2019 report of the UN’s scientific panel, the Earth’s current atmosphere is on course to reach higher levels of 1.5°C or 2.7°F within the next 20 years. The apprehension is that once the Earth’s temperature reaches 2°C, the occurrence could trigger a global food crisis.</p>
<p>The United States is the only country to have taken a step backward by withdrawing from the pact.That is notwithstanding the fact the U.S is one of the major contributors of greenhouse gas emissions that continue to worsen the effects of climate change. This was stated in the 2018 report submitted to the U.N. by lead author Natalie Mahowald, a climate scientist at Cornell University.</p>
<h3><strong>Why Did Trump Withdraw from the Paris Climate Accord?</strong></h3>
<p>First off, Donald Trump is admittedly not a believer in science. This was quite evident on how he <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/03/17/covid-19-pandemic-a-scientific-prediction-proven-true/">disregarded the warnings and advice of science experts on steps to take in order to overcome the strickening effects of the COVID-19 pandemic</a> </strong>throughout the country.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/vlXf0ip7pt4?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="480" title="Rejoining Paris Climate Accord Would Be Easy for Biden But Disaster Risk Remains" width="640"></iframe></p>

<div class="entry-content">
<p>As vaccine manufacturers are undertaking clinical tests to prove the efficiency of their anti <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/07/20/is-it-alright-for-us-to-treat-the-covid-19-crisis-as-a-political-matter/">COVID-19 drugs, vaccine distribution has become a political issue.</a></strong> The term “vaccine nationalism” has been brought up by the World Health Organization (WHO) and WHO Chief Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus is calling on rich nations to stop putting their own interests ahead of other nations. He warns that vaccine “hoarding” can only make the pandemic worse.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/cIfFDg2lXeI?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="Clinical trials to test COVID-19 vaccine underway in the Triad" width="640"></iframe></p>
<h3><strong>Why the WHO is Calling on Rich Nations to Refrain from Practicing Vaccine Nationalism</strong></h3>
<p>In a world of roughly 8 billion people, there is a growing fear that the <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/07/17/lets-stop-playing-politics-distribution-plans-for-the-potential-corona-vaccine/">advance agreements to prioritize the domestic markets of rich nations will only make the COVID-19 vaccines unavailable and unaffordable to smaller and poorer countries.</a> </strong>Unlike the rich EU-member countries, the UK as well as the U.S , not all countries can afford the multi-billion pre-purchase deals entered into with leading vaccine manufacturers like AstraZeneca Plc, Johnson &amp; Johnson and Pfizer Inc, even before the clinical effectiveness have been proven..</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/PdEfoY4z6b4?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title='WHO warns against "vaccine nationalism"' width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>Still, in order not to let vaccine nationalism put smaller and poorer countries at a disadvantage, the WHO has established a Covax Facility. Together with the Coalition for Epidemic Preparedness Innovations, the Covax Facility intends to purchase two billion doses of COVID-19 vaccines, which the WHO will deploy and distribute across low income and poorer countries. However, the initiative is projected to actually roll out by the end of next year.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/UGgnGwQpBeI?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="‘Vaccine nationalism only helps the virus’: WHO on global Covid-19 vaccine plan" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>In the UK, an anti-poverty group called ONE Campaign commissioned a polling firm to find out how Britons feel about vaccine nationalism. Conducted sometime in mid-August, the poll surveys showed a majority of those who responded to the survey do not approve of vaccine nationalism. The Director of ONE Campaign Romilly Greenhill, said the poll results show that the British people clearly understand the need to address a global pandemic with a global response, instead of each country going their own separate ways.</p>

<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/07/15/how-the-general-public-can-influence-political-decisions/" rel="bookmark">How the General Public can Influence Political Decisions?</a></h2> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p><strong><em>Most countries are <a href="/2020/02/05/why-iowas-democratic-caucus-is-taking-long-to-produce-100-voting-results/">following a democratic government</a></em></strong>, which means, the nation is ruled by its people. Therefore, it is essential that the citizens have a say in what’s being decided and what will happen. Politics is all around us; whether it is in the government, in business competition such as <em><u>removalist Perth</u></em> and everything in between. Decisions that these politicians make will be affecting our lives from commuting, schools, shopping and so forth.</p>
<p><img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-500 lazyload" data-src="http://worldnewspolitics.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/politics.jpg" data-srcset="http://worldnewspolitics.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/politics.jpg 665w, http://worldnewspolitics.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/politics-300x203.jpg 300w" height="451" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 665px) 100vw, 665px" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20665%20451%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" width="665"/></p>
<p>With this in mind, how people can actually influence what’s being decided? In reality, there are more ways than one that this can be done and these include:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Voting,</strong></li>
<li><strong>Campaigning,</strong></li>
<li><strong>Signing a petition</strong></li>
<li><strong>Joining political parties and</strong></li>
<li><strong>Becoming involved in pressure groups</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>Let try to discuss some of these approaches even further.</p>
<h3><strong>Voting</strong></h3>
<p>This is actually among the <em>significant ways in which people or the public can take part in a decision. </em>Usually, they do this during election period when the entire country is given the power to select who they wanted to represent them.</p>
<p><strong>Campaigning</strong></p>
<p>With regards to campaigning, this may actually <strong>involve a number of different approaches</strong> in an effort to persuade the general public.</p>
<blockquote><p><em>People may be involved by sending letters, demonstrations, emails, organizing visits to the political party, petitions or by organizing media as well as social media campaigns.</em></p></blockquote>
<h3><strong>Petitions</strong></h3>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Aba_kQu1sB0?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="What is a Petition?" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>In most cases, petitions are being used in election campaigns as a way of <strong>showing the level of support for a certain point of view</strong>. Normally, they do contain statements regarding the objective and the goal of the campaign. Furthermore, the campaigners are seeking to get additional support by asking others to sign in their petition.</p>
<p>Each and every single petition that the parliament obtains should be looked by a governing body. This entity will be deciding whether the petition is within the parliament’s power and if yes, they ought to <u>investigate the concern and make recommendations of the appropriate steps to be taken</u>.</p>
<h3><strong>Who is Petitioning the Parliament?</strong></h3>
<p>As a matter of fact, anyone has the right to petition the parliament. Petitions may be submitted either by a single individual or a group of people or even organized pressure group.</p>

<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/05/10/nz-pm-led-country-out-of-covid-19-pandemic/" rel="bookmark">NZ PM Led Country Out of COVID -19 Pandemic</a></h2> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p>New Zealand’s Prime Minister Jacinta Arden, brings hope to many across the globe and pride to her countrymen for leading them out of the <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/03/17/covid-19-pandemic-a-scientific-prediction-proven-true/">COVID-19 pandemic</a></strong>. To date (May 09, 2020), New Zealand is currently dealing with only 1,144 cases of COVID-19 infection, while 1, 371 of previous reported cases have recovered, and only 21 have succumbed to death, since the first confirmed case was reported in Feb.28, 2020.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ShWPNtell50?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="PM Jacinda Ardern explains Covid-19 lockdown in NZ" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>While the world is constantly shocked by news about U.S. president <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2019/05/18/political-justification-no-excuse-for-a-shameful-act/">Trump’s blatant lies and self-congratulatory speeches</a></strong> about actions taken by his administration in battling the coronavirus disease, the U.S. COVID-19 statistics continue to rise.</p>
<p>Currently, the total number of U.S. residents infected has reached more than 1.34 million, with around 201,000 reported recoveries and a rapidly rising death toll of more than 81,000; making the country the epicenter of the global pandemic since the first case of locally transmitted infection was reported in January 2020.</p>
<p>It cannot be helped that comparisons are being made since the way things are in the U.S. are hard to accept as signs that everything is turning out well for the American people. Whereas in New Zealand, lockdown conditions have eased down from Level 4 to Level 3. Last April 20, 2020, PM Arden had announced:</p>
<blockquote>
<h4><strong>“We have won the battle…“There is no widespread undetected community transmission in New Zealand, but we have to remain vigilant if we are to keep it that way.”</strong></h4>
</blockquote>
<h3><strong>What Does Level 3 Lockdown Mean in NZ?</strong></h3>
<p>The Level 3 lockdown in NZ took effect last April 27, 2020, in which stay-at-home restrictions have been modified, while safe distancing practices must still be observed. Although working from home and online learning is still encouraged, schools, some nonessential businesses and offices are allowed to open as long as measures for keeping a safe distance of at least 6 feet apart will be practiced.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/SrVIZ8O5KHg?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="New Zealanders rush for fast food as COVID-19 lockdown eases" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>There is also more leeway under Level 3, as NZ residents can go out to take a swim, hike, bike, surf or fish as long as people will not venture far from their home. Although mass gatherings are still not allowed, New Zealnaders can now hold weddings and even funerals; but limited to 10 people in attendance.</p>
<h3><strong>PM Arden’s Decisive Actions and Straightforward Messages Made the Big Difference</strong></h3>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/9bxtL9VnfMQ?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="New Zealand to keep many restrictions in place even after lockdown eases" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>When PM Arden announced the Level 4 nationwide lockdown in March, her message to the people of New Zealand was that they will °go hard, and go early.” She immediately tasked members of the country’s military forces to take charge in enforcing the hard lockdown restrictions.</p>
<p>PM Arden did not mince words and was tough when necessary. As a matter of, she did not hesitate to demote a health minister who openly disregarded the government’s safe distancing mandates. In a matter of 4 weeks, Prime Minister Arden announced that the collective efforts of millions of New Zealanders had paid off, as they have succeeded in achieving what other countries are still trying to achieve up to this date. That of “flattening the curve” and more since local transmissions have been brought down to zero level.</p>

<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/02/05/why-iowas-democratic-caucus-is-taking-long-to-produce-100-voting-results/" rel="bookmark">Why Iowa’s Democratic Caucus is Taking Long to Produce 100% Voting Results</a></h2> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p>Results of the Iowa Democratic caucus keep trickling nearly 2 days after the conventions were held. As of this writing 71%, representing 1,250 of 1,765 precincts that have sent in results, have placed Mayor Pete Buttigieg in the lead at 26.8%. Senator Bernie Sanders is not far behind, lagging by only 1.6%.</p>
<p>The smartphone app that could have speeded up the results-counting process did not help at all. Apparently, there was some sort of glitch, which app developer Shadow Inc. discovered only on Caucus Day.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/kbrZb2XxF3I?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="Iowa Democratic Chair Defends Integrity Of Caucus Data: 'This Is Personal To Me' | NBC News" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>Actually, Mayor Buttigieg’s lead is representative of the number of state delegates who voted for his nomination, and not the raw votes or the number of people who voted for a preferred Democratic presidential candidate for 2020.</p>
<p>It’s all a bit confusing actually, since the <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2019/04/28/the-significance-of-political-science/">caucus method of selecting the presidential candidate is different from the primary election approach.</a></strong> Iowa is one of few states that still select a presidential nominee by way of caucus voting.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/eFTGmG_sMXE?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="Democratic candidates react to Iowa caucus confusion" width="640"></iframe></p>
<h3><strong>The Basics of the Caucus Method of Nominating a Presidential Candidate of a Party</strong></h3>
<p>As it is, the Democratic Party has about 12 candidates aspiring for nomination as presidential candidate to run against Republican Party candidate <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2019/06/18/u-s-president-defends-his-penis-size/">Donald Trump.</a></strong></p>
<p>The caucus method though is a bit confusing, because unlike in the primary election system adopted by other U.S. states, registered voters do not cast votes directly for their preferred presidential candidate.</p>
<p>At an appointed date, time, and place, any registered voter of a specific party can attend the convention or meeting to vote for a delegate .The delegates in turn represent voters in nominating the presidential candidate during the Caucus Convention. Here there will be two rounds of voting for the nomination.</p>
<p>In the first round the chosen delegates cast votes for the presidential candidate they support as nominee. The weakest presidential candidate or those who garnered numbers of votes that are less than the threshold, will be eliminated as choices in the second or final round of voting.</p>
<p>However, during the second-round voting, the delegates who supported the weak candidates will still participate by voting for a second-choice presidential candidate of the registered voters they represent. Prior to the final round, delegates will discuss and debate as a way of trying to win over the support of delegates voting for a second choice.</p>
<p>In understanding the caucus method of voting, we get a better picture of why it takes long before Iowa Democrats could produce a 100% report of the state’s voting results. Inasmuch as there is still 29% remaining, and only a 1.6% difference between Mayor Buttigieg and Senator Sanders, the final result of the Iowa caucus is still not final unless all precincts have sent in the results of their caucus-voting.</p>

<div class="entry-content">
<p><strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2019/09/01/the-influence-of-the-camera-in-presenting-politicians/">House Republicans pulled a stunt</a></strong> last Wednesday (Oct. 23, 2019) in order to disrupt a closed-door hearing in which high level diplomats and government officials were scheduled to give their testimony to the House impeachment inquiry headed by Democratic House Representative Adam Schiff, Chairman of the House Intelligence Committee.</p>
<p>Around 2 dozen Republican lawmakers surged past Capitol Hill police officers and overcame Democratic staffers; whilst compromising the security of the Sensitive Compartmented Information Facility (SCIF), where the closed-door testimonials were scheduled to be heard.</p>
<p>Obviously carried out as a way of showing support for Donald Trump, some were literally shouting at the top of their lungs to attract attention, so that Trump will later see them showing solid opposition against the ongoing impeachment proceedings. Some even went in carrying electronic devices, tweeting about their “storming” of the secured room. North Carolina Republican House Rep. Mark Walker’s tweet read</p>
<blockquote>
<h4><strong>“UPDATE: We are in the SCIF and every GOP Member is quietly listening.”</strong></h4>
</blockquote>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/fzBG32UXVOI?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="House Republicans storm closed-door impeachment deposition" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>Earlier in the week, House Republicans received urging from Donald Trump, by telling his Republican minions to stick together and fight harder. To make his message clear, Trump told them that the reason why House Democrats are more effective is because they do not <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2019/05/18/political-justification-no-excuse-for-a-shameful-act/">break ranks.</a></strong></p>
<p>Naturally, Trump was pleased by the chaos created by the group of House Republicans in the Wednesday closed-door hearings. <p>Apparently, the House Republicans who stormed the SCIF, were either unmindful or did not have a clear understanding of the related Congressional House rules.</p>
<h3><strong>Congressional House Rules with Regard to Closed-Door Hearings</strong></h3>
<p>First off, the Republicans are overlooking the fact that the purpose for holding closed-door sessions is to make sure that the testimonies of different witnesses will not be influenced by the information provided by earlier witnesses.</p>
<p>During the closed-door session, both parties, Republicans and Democrats alike are represented through lawmakers who have been designated as members of the house committee holding the hearings.</p>
<p>Copies of transcripts detailing the sworn statement or deposition of the witnesses who gave their testimony during the hearing will be released after the closed-door session. House non committee-members excluded from the hearing will have a chance to question the closed-door witnesses in public, at the conclusion of the investigative stage.</p>
<p>Closed-door hearings are held in a Sensitive Compartmented Information Facility (SCIF) to ensure that only committee members will be privy to any sensitive or classified information that will be given. This is also the reason why cellphones or any electronic devices are not allowed so as not to compromise or allow potential breach of information related to national security.</p>
<h3><strong>Who Wrote and Approved the House Rules for Closed Door Hearings?</strong></h3>
<p>Judge Andrew Peter Napolitano who served as Supreme Court Judge for New Jersey from 1987 to 1995, and currently engaged as an analyst for Fox News, pointed out important information about the closed-door House Rules being protested by Republican lawmakers.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/73fWsWh0aNU?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="House Speaker John Boehner scolds lawmakers on dress code" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>The rules were last written in January 2015, and was signed by Republican House Representative John Boehner, in his capacity as the 53rd Speaker of the U.S. House of Representatives. The rules were enacted by a Republican majority.</p>

<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/09/12/importance-of-adequate-sleep/" rel="bookmark">Importance Of Adequate Sleep</a></h2> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p><strong>The demands and pressures of being a political leader may take a toll on the person’s sleep and rest.</strong> Former president Barack Obama, for instance, says he schedules at least six hours of sleep every night. However, this isn’t always achievable with the grueling schedules of being a president.</p>
<h2><img alt="" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-870 lazyload" data-src="http://worldnewspolitics.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/sleep-300x200.jpg" data-srcset="http://worldnewspolitics.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/sleep-300x200.jpg 300w, http://worldnewspolitics.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/sleep.jpg 640w" height="200" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20300%20200%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" width="300"/>Importance of Sleep</h2>
<p>For any political leader or for any individual in every walk of life,<strong> having adequate sleep and rest is crucial for one to function at an optimal level.</strong> However, sleep doesn’t come fast and easy for many which results in inadequate rest and exhaustion.</p>
<p><strong>Providentially, there are sleeping aids that could help people fall asleep faster as well as improve the quality of sleep.</strong> For instance, Hibernate, a bodybuilding sleep supplement made from various herbs, minerals and vitamins is formulated to give body builders as well as fitness enthusiasts that sleep and rest needed for muscle recovery. Moreover, this bodybuilding sleep supplement also helps in improving the quality of sleep of users to promote muscle growth.</p>
<h3>Benefits of Adequate Sleep</h3>
<p>Severe sleep deprivation is something that political leaders should avoid. <strong>As the political landscape often entails important <a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/07/15/how-the-general-public-can-influence-political-decisions/" title="How the General Public can Influence Political Decisions?">decision making</a> processes, one should have good cognitive functioning so as to avoid any errors or mistakes.</strong> When one is sleep deprived, they become irritable, experience mood swings and this impairs cognitive functioning, communication, coordination, and most importantly their capacity to make judicious decisions. For example, individuals who are deprived of sleep become less flexible and less able to handle or deal with unexpected and sudden changes in set plans or course of action.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/dqONk48l5vY?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="What would happen if you didn’t sleep? - Claudia Aguirre" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>With that, it is then imperative not only for political leader but for everyone to get enough sleep and rest to reap the many benefits it offers. Below are some:</p>
<ul>
<li>Better focus and increased productivity</li>
<li>Improved emotional and social intelligence</li>
<li>Improved physical and mental performance</li>
<li>Better regulation of calories which lowers risk of weight gain</li>
<li>Prevents stress, anxiety and depression</li>
<li>Improved immune system</li>
</ul>
<p>According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), sleep quantity varies by age so as to have better and proper physical and psychological functioning.</p>
<ul>
<li>0 to 3 months old: 14 to 17 hours</li>
<li>4 to 12 months old: 12 to 16 hours</li>
<li>1 to 2 years old: 11 to 14 hours</li>
<li>3 to 5 years old: 10 to 13 hours</li>
<li>6 to 12 years old: 9 to 12 hours</li>
<li>13 to 18 years old: 8 to 10 hours</li>
<li>18 to 60 years old: at least 7 hours</li>
<li>61 to 64 years old: 7 to 9 hours</li>
<li>65+ years old: 7 to 8 hours</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/09/10/wildlife-agency-considers-crossbows-as-archery-equipment/" rel="bookmark">Wildlife Agency Considers Crossbows as Archery Equipment</a></h2> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p>While issues are raised about calling crossbow sport as a form of archery, the U.S. wildlife agency says crossbows are archery equipment under taxation laws.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Fa03yBJ7tCI?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title='Archery | Are Crossbows Considered To Be "Bows"?' width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>Actually, the tax collected is by virtue of the Pittman-Robertson Act that includes crossbows among the archery shooting equipment subject to excise tax, regardless if for sporting competition or for hunting use. Part of the taxes collected are turned over by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) to the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, which redistributes them as supplementary funds of state wildlife agencies.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/1mwWExEBAQQ?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="Wildlife Habitat Management" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>On a state level, the funds are provided to local wildlife agencies to help them manage and restore wildlife habitats, conduct wildlife research, offer hunter education and public access programs, as well as carry out high-priority national wildlife conservation projects.</p>
<p>That being the case, the Archery Trade Association (ATA) considers crossbows as viable hunting and shooting equipment, to which a related definition was ratified by the ATA Board of Directors in March 2016. That way, archery merchants have a clear guide in knowing that crossbows are among the archery equipment subject to Federal Excise Tax (FET).</p>
<h3><strong>The ATA Definition Recognizes the Similarity of Crossbows with Other Archery Equipment</strong></h3>
<p>The ATA definition acknowledges that as a type of archery equipment, a crossbow does not require the user to exert personal force to keep the string in a fully drawn position after the string has been engaged into a trigger mechanism; and that a crossbow is fired once the user actuates that mechanism.</p>
<p>The trade association’s crossbow definition also recognizes its similarity to other archery equipment, by stating that the</p>
<blockquote>
<h4><strong>”The actuation of a crossbow’s trigger mechanism releases the string, while the arrow, quarrel or bolt is propelled forward by the resiliency and elastic characteristic of the crossbow’s limbs.” “The propelling force created by the trigger transfers to the arrow, quarrel or bolt by way of direct physical contact with the crossbow string.”</strong></h4>
</blockquote>
<h3><strong>Implication of the FET Tax Among Crossbow Users</strong></h3>
<p>When searching for the <strong>best crossbows on the market</strong>, be in the know that the retail prices of all archery equipment include FET taxes.</p>
<p>Actually, payment of excise taxes starts with manufacturers who are required to pay 10 to 11 % tax on the first sale of hunting and shooting equipment, including ammunition. Manufacturers then pass on the FET they paid to wholesalers, who in turn do the same by passing on the FET to retailers. The latter will then be the last channel by which the related excise taxes are passed on to the end users or consumers, by way of retail prices. .</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/2H4rejXWZks?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="480" title="Federal Excise Taxes – Where do they go?" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>The Federal Excise Tax on hunting and shooting equipment was legislated by Congress in 1937, under <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/06/25/the-importance-of-citing-government-documents/">the Federal Aid in Wildlife Restoration Act, which is also known as the Pittman-Robertson Ac</a>t.</strong> The inclusion of archery equipment and related ammunition was through an amendment legislated in 1972.</p>
<p>In 2003, the IRS updated the FET when it created two separate tax collection schemes for archery equipment:</p>
<p>1  One is the 11% FET imposed on bows, broadheads, quivers and other bow attachments or accessories.</p>
<p>2. The second is a flat rate tax on arrow shafts, but subject to annual adjustments based on the current Consumer Price Index; e.g. 50 cents per shaft.</p>
<p>ATA VP and Chief Conservation Officer Dan Forster explained that FETs <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/07/23/funding-for-elderly-care/">provide funds</a></strong> that state fish and wildlife agencies use to ensure that sports hunters and recreational shooters will continue to have professionally and scientifically managed places in which to hunt and pursue animals.</p>

<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/09/06/politics-and-the-aging-population/" rel="bookmark">Politics And The Aging Population</a></h2> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p><strong>The aging of the population is most often discussed as a welfare problem where older people appear as objects needing free</strong> <strong>arthrozene rather than important political actors. </strong>Here we will place the main emphasis on the actor perspective, but first a few words about whether longer lives and older populations will be able to have political consequences in a more indirect way.</p>
<h3>Aging Germany</h3>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/PXdz0m6-mrQ?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="Aging Germany - an overview | People &amp; Politics" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p><strong>It seems so in the sense that care for the elderly has gained a larger place on the political agenda in recent years.</strong> There has been a lot of political noise about the elderly policy, but in reality little controversy. The needs of older people have been seen with great sympathy in all political parties, which have almost competed to be friendly to the elderly, especially in speeches if not as clearly in action. The elderly issues also have great support and legitimacy in the population – also among younger people (Bay 1998, Aardal 1999). Similar patterns are found in other European countries (Walker 1993, Walker &amp; Naegele 1999). Population development is probably not the only explanation for this, but is an essential part of it.</p>
<p><strong>The support is particularly high for elderly care and especially care for those most in need of care (Bay 1998).</strong> In this way, nursing homes are stronger than old-age pensions both among politicians and in public opinion. It is primarily the weakness of old age that invites sympathy, compassion and solidarity. This is an example of how powerlessness can be a source of influence, at least when this powerlessness is seen as unjustified.</p>
<p><strong>The broad support for elderly measures in public opinion cannot be explained only by self-interest.</strong> Admittedly, older people are somewhat more likely to support such measures than younger people, but the differences are moderate. Elderly measures also have great legitimacy among young people; perhaps as much as child and youth measures have among the elderly. It’s hard to believe that younger people think about their own old age when they quit the policy of the elderly. Life phase interests probably come into play, but are not dominant (Rhodebeck 1993). Then the general norm of responsibility seems to be stronger – the obligation to help people in need. This can be about an altruistic attitude (towards anyone) or primarily about taking responsibility for those in need of help that one identifies with (solidarity).</p>
<p>The sympathy for <strong>the elderly policy</strong> also seems to draw nourishment from a tendency among most people to exaggerate the problems of the elderly (Bay 1998, Lødemel &amp; Flaa 1993). This is also the case among politicians, where the tendency to support transfers to the elderly increases with how great the needs are (Lubomudrow 1997).</p>
<p>The increased number of older people can also be expected to have a greater influence due to the fact that they make up an increasing proportion of the electorate. <strong>Politicians may be tempted to place greater emphasis on the interests of older people in order to attract older voters.</strong> This can be counteracted by the belief that older people are more loyal to their party than younger voters are. In that case, the focus is rather on the young voters, who also have the value that they have a longer future as voters.</p>
<p>All in all, the longer life and the increased weight of the elderly population have probably contributed to the needs and interests of old age becoming higher on the <a href="/">political agenda</a>, but then primarily the frailty of the fourth age. It has been more difficult to create interest in the third age. Politics and the media thus contribute to confirming the stereotypical image of aging as a weakening and older people as burdens. <strong>The poor political conditions for public investment have further contributed to weakening the position of the elderly and other clients in society.</strong> They appear as foreigners (the wave of the elderly) and as burdens for the younger part of the population (generational justice), which in the long run may weaken support for measures for the elderly.</p>

<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/07/20/is-it-alright-for-us-to-treat-the-covid-19-crisis-as-a-political-matter/" rel="bookmark">Is It Alright For Us To Treat The COVID-19 Crisis As A Political Matter?</a></h2> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p>It is a well-known fact by now that the <strong>novel coronavirus</strong> that originated from Wuhan, China impacts not just the physical body of its unwilling victims. The <strong>COVID-19 pandemic</strong> has forced industries to limit their operations, or worse, to shut down indefinitely, which puts millions of workers without income for several months. What’s scary is that the timing is not right for anyone to be <em>troubled financially</em> with the pandemic still raging on. The cost of hospitalization will be the patient’s number one problem, and without salary and health care benefits, he could only hope and pray that the disease would spare him.</p>
<blockquote><p>As the COVID-19 crisis is something that the ordinary citizens cannot solve by themselves, it is just normal for people to ask the government for help. In the first place, it is the government’s job to protect its citizens, especially when the situation gets out of hand.</p></blockquote>
<p>This is where we can have a judgement of our leaders’ response to the crisis, based on their words and actions. Although, some are worrying that the pandemic will turn into something that politicians can throw around during political debates.</p>
<p><img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-500 lazyload" data-src="http://worldnewspolitics.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/politics.jpg" data-srcset="http://worldnewspolitics.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/politics.jpg 665w, http://worldnewspolitics.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/politics-300x203.jpg 300w" height="451" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 665px) 100vw, 665px" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20665%20451%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" width="665"/></p>
<h3>The Pandemic Can Easily Be A Political Issue, Especially If The Situation Gets Much Worse</h3>
<p>For starters, we cannot control what the public is thinking about the COVID-19 response of the government. Current leaders lament that the pandemic is being used against them to discredit them. However, the public’s opinion is only based on their experience amid the pandemic, and how their situation is being helped (or worsened) by whatever the government is doing to address the situation. So in a nutshell, yes, the pandemic is a political matter, because this is a way for people to see what the politicians can actually do for them.</p>
<p>In a way, it is good for us, the ordinary people, that the COVID-19 pandemic has laid bare the real character of the politicians who have sworn during the election period to do the best that they could to protect the welfare of the public. Right now, we should take note of those people who are actually doing something to help the people. For one, making sure that the <a href="/lets-stop-playing-politics-distribution-plans-for-the-potential-corona-vaccine/">distribution of the potential coronavirus vaccine</a> will be in order must be one of our leader’s priorities.</p>

<p>Does the <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/03/13/small-to-medium-scale-businesses-are-placed-on-hold-due-to-virus/">Covid-19</a></strong> pandemic prove true a scientific prediction that once global warming thaws permafrost, spores of deadly diseases will be released in the air?</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/B8pSo3_0gkk?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="Could thawing permafrost unleash long-gone deadly viruses?" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>Climate studies conducted by scientists decades ago arrived at a prediction that if global warming intensifies, the thawing of thick layers of permafrost in Siberia, Alaska, Greenland and northern Canada, will become inevitable.</p>
<p>The permafrost in those regions are permanently frozen layers of soil that thickened continuously. The thickness allows the fossils of ancient plants and animals that perished during the Ice Age and buried underneath, to remain undisturbed for thousands of years.</p>
<p>Today, large areas of thick permafrosts in those regions have been thawing rapidly. In 2019, the National Geographic came out with a report that in Siberia, the permafrost in a cliff called Duvanny Yar had warmed and thawed. A visiting ecologist found that the crumbling soil of the cliff had spilled fossils of mammoth bones, horse femurs, bison jaws and other ancient matters into the sea.</p>
<p>This National Geographic report brought us back to an earlier Scientific American publication that gave news about a puzzling deadly disease that came around in a remote area in Siberia during the summer of 2016. The disease had claimed the lives of 20 people living in the remote area and more than 2,300 reindeers inhabiting nearby forests.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/OfpnbBqhZLk?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="A Reindeer Might Have Caused Siberia's Sudden Anthrax Outbreak" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>The cause of the infectious disease was a bacterium known as bacillus anthracis, from which the deadly contagion known as Anthrax was derived. The source of the bacillus anthracis bacteria was the large amount of fossils coming out of the thawed permafrost located in the region.</p>
<h3><strong>Scientific Views on the Re-Emergence of Infectious Diseases in the Modern World</strong></h3>
<p>Astrologers usually make predictions of plagues using the ancient Babylonian astrologers’ method of interpreting <strong>zodiac stars</strong> in relation to past events. The underlying belief of ancient Babylonian astrology is that everything that happens on Earth has happened before, and that it is bound to happen again at a predetermined time in the future.</p>
<p>There is some semblance of truth to this theory since modern-day scientists have established the fact that our planet’s functions and systems usually work as a cycle.<br/>
The re-emergence of bacteria and viruses that cause infectious diseases is seemingly a part of those cycles.</p>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/X31g5TB-MRo?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="Where Did Viruses Come From?" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p>Yet the world is also seeing the emergence of novel pathogens, like the Covid-19 infectious disease that communities all over the world are battling today.</p>
<p>According to infectious disease researchers Mark Woolhouse and Eleanor Gaunt of the University of Edinburgh, the world can be expected to produce new infectious-disease causing pathogens at an estimated rate of three species per year, because of the ongoing global <strong><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2019/08/22/democratic-sen-chris-coons-say-some-gop-senators-are-showing-signs-of-supporting-climate-change-actions/">ecological changes</a></strong>. They drew this general conclusion based on a study that involved creating a catalogue of nearly 1,400 species of novel human pathogens and their ecological origins.</p>

<div class="entry-content">
<p>Many <strong>small to medium scale companies throughout Asia and Europe</strong> are feeling the impact of the coronavirus on the economy. Many business storage facilities in the U.K. facilities are filled to the ceiling waiting for dispatch.</p>
<p><strong>In Korea, companies that produce packaging boxes in Incheon have recently lost 25% of their sales.</strong> Because domestic companies <a href="/2019/04/05/chinas-diplomacy-tactics-debt-trap-diplomacy/">exporting to China</a> have reduced box orders. “This situation has significantly reduced exports to China, reducing the number of box orders,” said Kumo, the company’s CEO.</p>
<h2 class="title style-scope ytd-video-primary-info-renderer"><strong>How the coronavirus is impacting the global economy</strong></h2>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/hwQ3KyxMSkE?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="How the coronavirus is impacting the global economy" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p><strong>Recently, the spread of new coronavirus infections is rapidly increasing the number of small and medium-sized enterprises that are suffering from economic hardship.</strong> The SMEs conducted an emergency investigation against 250 small and medium-sized companies that have dealt with China or established subsidiaries in China. As a result, 34.4% of respondents said they were directly hit by the situation.</p>
<p><strong>The supply and demand disruption was the highest at 56.4%.</strong> As the prices of some raw materials, which are difficult to supply and demand, rise, the burden on the companies is also increasing. As the demand for disinfecting products increased due to the spread of new corona, the price of ethanol, a raw material for detergents, jumped. Even if there are funds to produce disinfecting products, there is still a need to secure ethanol as a raw material to go on with production.</p>
<p><strong>In Japan, auto parts vendors are also concerned about the lack of inventory due to prolonged events.</strong> Chairman of the Japan Automobile Parts Sales Cooperative, said, “In recent years, the new corona has reduced the industry’s sales by 20 to 30% compared to normal.”</p>
<p><strong>The difficulty of small business owners is more serious.</strong> Most of the small business owners are experiencing a drop in sales due to the reluctance to go out or gather due to concerns about coronavirus infection. Recently, a small business association surveyed 1092 small business owners and found that 97.9% of all respondents said their sales fell due to the new corona. The decline in sales was also large. 44% of respondents (480) said their sales fell more than 50%. 27.2% (296) answered that sales were reduced by 30-50%.</p>
<p>61.4% of respondents (667) responded indefinitely due to the indefinite cancellation for <strong>various gatherings, events, and travel</strong>. Also, 22.5% (245) of respondents said they are suffering from a decrease in the floating population because of the new corona definite path of movement.</p>

<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/02/24/beijing-citizens-need-air-purifiers-more-than-anyone-else/" rel="bookmark">Beijing Citizens Need Air Purifiers More Than Anyone Else</a></h2> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p><strong>It cannot be denied that the senior officials of the CCP have always enjoyed certain “special privileges.”</strong> These privileges include state-sponsored limousines, special schools for high-ranking children, and even organic vegetables picked from farmland that is closely guarded and operated by the government. When they are ill, senior officials can go to the 301 Army General Hospital to check their health. The 301 has long been regarded as a high-level medical institution in the capital of Beijing.</p>
<h2 class="title style-scope ytd-video-primary-info-renderer">Air pollution chokes Beijing</h2>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/BaFf82vXXXs?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="Air pollution chokes Beijing" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p><strong>Ordinary Beijing residents are jealous.</strong> When the pollution is particularly serious, the air they breathe into their lungs is no different from those of high-ranking and powerful people.</p>
<p><strong>This idea does not seem entirely accurate.</strong></p>
<p>The fact is that many high-end homes and offices are equipped with<strong> first-class air filtration devices</strong> (see The Best Air Purifier Reviews in 2020), and at least one Chinese company, Broad Group, specifically advertised in its air purifiers that it emphasizes that many officials are equipped where they live and work.</p>
<p>Zhang Zhong, the company’s deputy general manager, said that there were more than 200 installations in the Great Hall of the People, in the office of Chinese President Hu Jintao, and in Zhongnanhai, where the senior leaders and their families live. Taiwan ’s air purifiers, <em><strong>“Providing clean and healthy air to national leaders is a blessing to the people.”</strong></em> This company ’s promotional materials are so complimented that they also include a series of government and business leaders ’ Proof, including the words of Long Yongtu, a senior economic official who insisted on having this device in his car and hotel stay. He said “Breathing clean air is a basic need of man.”</p>
<p><strong>In some countries, providing a series of testimony from senior officials is considered an effective means of good public relations.</strong> However, when dissatisfaction and anger rose, news reports triggered by the company’s advertising campaign stirred a storm of criticism. “They don’t have to eat trench oil or drink poisonous milk, and now they don’t have to breathe contaminated air.” A Sina Weibo post says this, which is China’s most popular Weibo service platform. “It shows that they have no sympathy for the lives of ordinary people.”</p>
<p><strong>News that Chinese leaders can be spared Beijing’s harsh air has made the rare appearance of heavy pollution in Beijing.</strong> In recent weeks, Beijing has been shrouded in light brown smoke, and air monitoring equipment from the roof of the US embassy has always indicated that air indicators are at dangerous levels.</p>
<blockquote><p><em>The data is posted on Twitter via an iPhone app every hour, but it has sparked a public debate about whether the Chinese government is intentionally vague about China’s air pollution levels.</em></p></blockquote>
<p><strong>Unlike data from the U.S. Embassy, ​​Chinese environmental officials did not disclose data on the smallest particulate pollutants.</strong> Scientists say that particles smaller than 2.5 microns are the most harmful because they can penetrate deeply and be sucked into the lungs. The government’s data only reports pollutant particles larger than 10 microns-this category includes dust storms blowing from the north and particles caused by dust from construction sites.</p>
<p><strong>Environmental officials like to focus on improving air quality</strong>, largely by replacing coal-fired stoves with electric stoves and shutting down heavy industries in and around Beijing. On Beijing’s already heavily congested roads, 700,000 new vehicles were added last year. These restrictions have slightly eased the environmental degradation caused by these vehicles.</p>
<p>But the same batch of officials acknowledged after their pressure that their pollution indicators consciously ignored smaller particles, which are mainly emitted from car and truck exhaust. In fact, according to a diplomatic report, <strong>the monitoring of the US embassy has become an unwelcome interference in China’s internal affairs</strong>, and officials at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs have demanded that Americans stop publishing the data.</p>
<p>The director of a Beijing-based non-profit organization, the Institute of Public Environmental Affairs, is concerned that public disclosure of this data could hinder development or shame the city’s image, as <strong>Beijing has been promoting environmental improvements</strong>.</p>
<blockquote><p><em>“I don’t agree with this logic,” said Director Ma Jun. “The more important thing for the government is to remind the public when air quality is harmful to vulnerable people, such as the elderly and children, so that they can be protected. “</em></p></blockquote>
<p><strong>The government seems to be improving in this direction.</strong> In September, the Ministry of <a href="/2020/01/04/the-world-keeps-vigil-on-australia-fires-and-pm-morrisons-actions/">Environmental Protection</a> said he planned to adjust the country’s air quality standards, including the disclosure of minimum particle targets, although it did not give a timetable for adopting the new standards.</p>
<p><strong>Beijing officials are clearly not ready to do so.</strong> In response to criticism of heavy fog in recent weeks, a spokesman for the city’s Ministry of Environmental Protection, Du Shaozhong, assured the public that they should be assured of government-provided indicators that Beijing’s air is only “lightly polluted “Even if monitoring by the U.S. Embassy believes that the level of harm is beyond measurable limits. “The data released by foreign embassies in Beijing should not be used to judge China’s air quality,” he said.</p>

<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://worldnewspolitics.com/2020/01/26/how-the-new-bill-would-affect-e-scooters-in-ireland/" rel="bookmark">How The New Bill Would Affect e-Scooters In Ireland</a></h2> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p><strong>A new bill submitted by Fianna Fáil might be regarding new laws for electric scooters. This includes the speed limit of such small vehicles.</strong></p>
<p>A bill that Fianna Fáil would bring to the Dáil later tonight (October 16) could turn the gray part of e-scooter legislation to black-and-white that’s more clear for everyone. <strong>It anticipates the summary of a consultation from the public on e-scooters made on 31 October and was released by the government following suggestions from the Road Safety Authority.</strong></p>
<p>As stipulated on the current Road Traffic Act of 1961, electric scooters are viewed as<strong> “mechanically driven vehicle”.</strong> Therefore, these small vehicles are supposed to be taxed and mandated to have insurance just like any cars, trucks or motorcycles on Irish roads.</p>
<p>As per the spokesperson for Transport, Tourism, and Sport, the suggested Bill would change the known definition of what is known as a mechanically driven vehicle to exempt <a href="/2019/07/30/future-of-hoverboards-rolab/">e-scooters and e-bikes</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Electric scooters are being used by many young people.</strong> This vehicle also offers an environmentally friendly alternative for many commuters who go on short journeys throughout the city.</p>
<h2>How The New Bill Would Affect e-Scooters In Ireland</h2>
<p><iframe allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" class="lazyload" data-src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/eypB_sfITHI?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" height="360" title="Electric Scooters And The Law In Ireland" width="640"></iframe></p>
<p><strong>Irish examiners have reported some of these recommendations, including speed limits, fines and their use in specific areas.</strong> This would make an impact to present e-scooter users.</p>
<p>The very first of these recommendations is a built-in speed limit of 25kph for electric scooters being posted for sale in Ireland, like those from xiaomi scooter sale. Those who alter the built-in speed limiter will be fined up to 2500 Euros. On the other hand, Mac Sharry suggested that the speeding fine should be consistent with current speeding fines of about 60 Euros.</p>
<p><strong>Electric scooter owners must also stay in the bike lane to share space with bike commuters.</strong> Cyclist. Mick McKillen acknowledges that sharing space with these new vehicles can lead to some traffic jams, but it will be beneficial for everyone after some time.</p>
<blockquote><p><em>He said “Every one scooter on the road means would mean one car less. From the climate action point of view, there’s a need to take people out of their cars.”</em></p></blockquote>
<p>Passing electric scooter legislation may see many companies providing ride-sharing services in the country.</p>
