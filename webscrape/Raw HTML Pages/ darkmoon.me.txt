<h6><em>DARKMOON  SITE  RECOVERS  FROM  SERIOUS  SETBACK</em></h6>
<p><strong>Dear Friends,</strong></p>
<p><strong>The site appears to be running still, with comments still tricking in, but I and my colleagues have been denied all access to the site since 16 March when disaster struck. An investigation is being carried out into what actually went on. </strong><span id="more-153952"></span></p>
<p>I prefer not to speculate at present or indulge in needlessly alarmist scenarios, but I  sincerely believe that there has been a serious <strong><em>technical breakdown</em></strong>.</p>
<p>Hacking, I am reliably informed by the skilled computer  technician who has helped to restore order, is most unlikely.  No one’s private details have been compromised in any way because no one has broken into our site. Every single document that was there prior to 16 March is <em>still</em> there. Intact. There have been no deletions or damage done  to any documents whatever.  Our readers and commenters can rest easy on that score.</p>
<p>New articles will be uploaded soon, and it’s hoped that everything will be back to normal by the end of the week. Quite a few readers will have abandoned the site, thinking it has been shut down or seriously disabled in some way. Maybe they will return in due course. If not, too bad.</p>
<p>Special thanks to Lobro for this kindly suggestion of his:</p>
<blockquote><p><strong>Do EVERYTHING possible to persuade your ex-webmaster to return to his job, however briefly, in order to clean the site up and upgrade various services. If you are short of funds to cover his fees, let the commentariat know, you can count on my help.</strong></p></blockquote>
<p>Fortunately, this won’t be necessary. We will not be asking our readers for donations. We are a small family concern and have managed so far to refuse all advertisements and even decline unsolicited donations from wellwishers — on one occasion, about five years ago,  a surprisingly generous donation of $7,500 from a wealthy Franco-German lady.</p>
<p>If people wish to be generous, however, I would suggest a small donation once a year at Christmas to a suitable children’s charity in  Occupied Palestine.</p>
<p>Thank you for reading this announcement. Comments are welcome. You will pardon me if I do not respond to questions. I am unable to do so until a proper  investigation has been carried out.</p>
<p>Kind regards,</p>
<p>Lasha Darkmoon</p>

<p><strong>The Biden Administration is now preparing to go after the people that it objects to and will create new laws as necessary to do so.</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong><em>By</em> Philip Giraldi</strong><br/>
<strong>March 12, 2021</strong><br/>
<span style="color: #000000;"><a href="https://www.strategic-culture.org/news/2021/03/11/domestic-terrorism-goes-transnational-war-dissidents-picks-up-momentum/" style="color: #000000;"><b>Strategic Culture Foundation via ICH</b></a></span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>THE  NEW  ENEMY  OF  THE  BIDEN  REGIME:</strong><br/>
<strong>A  HAPPY  WHITE  AMERICAN  FAMILY</strong></p>
<p><strong>The claim is often made that President George W. Bush’s war on terror, which produced legislation that was employed to attack Iraq in 2003, eventually morphed into the worst foreign policy mistake in U.S. history when that conflict destabilized the entire region and led to an American multifront military engagement that now appears permanent. </strong></p>
<p>Few of those in the policymaking business appreciated that by turning “terrorism” into an especially invidious form of evil allowing governments to arrest or even assassinate without due process and bomb civilians if they fit a profile, Pandora’s box was being opened to expand that authority to commit other heinous abuses of authority.</p>
<p>Jim Bovard has described how post 9/11 there were hundreds of arrests for no good reason, in some cases only because someone had a name or countenance that appeared to be “Arabic.” Congressman Ron Paul and a handful of others observed at the time that the legislation would inevitably be used against domestic enemies of the state as well as against foreign or foreign-linked groups, meaning that the real damage done by the Patriot Act, the Authorization to Use Military Force (AUMF) and the Military Commissions Act would be felt somewhere down the road, possibly at a point where the original objective of the legislation would be more or less forgotten.</p>
<blockquote><p><strong>Now that we have an identified “domestic terror” problem one should expect at a minimum a massive increase in surveillance of innocent citizens couple with arbitrary arrests and incarcerations. Indeed, the process is already well underway with FBI Director Christopher Wray announcing that there are several thousand terror “cases” under development. There will also be increasing calls to take away guns and to control what is allowed to appear on the internet. Soon Americans will have nothing to measure their remaining liberties by and will be less free to exercise rights including free speech, possibly dramatically so.</strong></p></blockquote>
<p>So now we have reached a point where we have a government that is committed to further reducing one’s rights in order to “keep us safe” from a domestic threat and congress critters are openly speaking of bringing in “war on terror” type expedients to make sure that they have the tools available to do just that.</p>
<p>The Joe Biden White House has made clear that it has embraced fighting domestic terrorists as a top priority. Last week, the Administration sought authorization from the Pentagon to keep thousands of national guard troops in the District of Columbia for 60 days more, presumably to protect the government buildings and staff. The pretext for the continued presence was a vaguely described plot constituting a “potential threat” to overrun the Capitol building on March 4th, a day when it was apparently anticipated that Donald Trump would miraculously be returned to office.</p>
<p>The House of Representatives even canceled a session over concerns that they were about to be invaded by a hostile “militia.” Just how “real” the threat was has not been made clear beyond suggestions of “chatter” over the internet, nor has there been any explanation of why the 2,200 strong Capitol Police force is unable to deal with the problem.</p>
<p>Be that as it may, the Biden Administration thinks it knows exactly who the enemy is. The government already has a working definition of a domestic terrorist, i.e. <em><strong>“If you advocate violence as a tool to further political ends, and take concrete steps to do that, you’re a terrorist.”</strong> </em>But if you thought that included groups like Antifa and Black Lives Matter (BLM) you would be wrong. For the Biden Administration it is the stereotyped right-wing extremist, who, among other attributes, is represented by the media and government as coming from the class that Hillary Clinton once described as “deplorables.”</p>
<blockquote><p><strong>The accepted definition of the enemy defies logic as the rioting, arson, and killing that has taken place over the past year has generally been inspired by Antifa and BLM, resulting in major damage and destruction in various cities and states. But the mobs who wrecked and looted have been mostly set free by the courts in the Democratic Party dominated cities. In Portland Oregon 90% of the rioters were not prosecuted, presumably because the local judicial system believed that their “cause was just.” </strong></p></blockquote>
<p>Against that is the trauma of the January 6th incident at the Capitol, much smaller in scope and damages but obviously terrifying to the media and Congress.</p>
<p>Also what did occur bore a more comfortable theme for the Democrats which they have been beating to death ever since – “insurrection caused by right wing extremists who were overwhelmingly white and support Donald Trump.”</p>
<p>That’s apparently all one needs to initiate a campaign to get rid of such dissidents.</p>
<p>For some suggestions about the direction the Biden Administration will be going in to eliminate domestic terrorism, one only has to review the comments of Attorney General nominee Merrick Garland at his Senate confirmation hearing on February 22nd, where he declared that going after domestic terrorists would be a top administration priority. When asked if he regards the numerous attempts by Antifa and BLM rioters to destroy federal courthouses in Portland and Seattle as acts of domestic extremism or terrorism, he hedged on the issue and provided no satisfactory answer.</p>
<p>According to Garland,  if you attack and seek to destroy a government building when there is no one in it, this  is a different level of criminality — <em>less criminal</em> — than seeking to disrupt business going on inside the building during business hours.</p>
<p>It seems pretty clear that the Biden Administration is now preparing to go after the people that it objects to and will create new laws as necessary to do so.</p>
<p>Garland will certainly have a hand in that development.</p>
<p>And if anyone is thinking of leaving all of this behind by fleeing to another country where there is an actual rule of law, it would be best to consider the matter again.</p>
<p>On February 22nd, United Nations Secretary-General Antonio Guterres warned that white supremacy right-wing nationalist movements have become a “transnational threat” that has exploited the fear of the coronavirus pandemic to gain support. He said that:</p>
<blockquote><p><strong>“White supremacy and neo-Nazi movements are more than domestic terror threats. They are becoming a transnational threat. Today, these extremist movements represent the number one internal security threat in several countries. Far too often, these hate groups are cheered on by people in positions of responsibility in ways that were considered unimaginable not long ago. [<em>A veiled reference to Donald Trump</em>]. We need global coordinated action to defeat this grave and growing danger.”</strong></p></blockquote>
<p>It means you can run but you can’t hide.</p>
<p>It looks like there will be a worldwide coalition to extirpate the evils that come automatically with whiteness and, as BLM is now de facto a major constituency of the U.S. Democratic Party,<strong> you know that Joe Biden and Nancy Pelosi will be leading the charge.</strong></p>
<p><img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-153839 lazyload ewww_webp_lazy_load" data-sizes="auto" data-src="https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1.jpeg" data-src-webp="https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1.jpeg.webp" data-srcset="https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1.jpeg 932w, https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1-300x169.jpeg 300w, https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1-150x84.jpeg 150w, https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1-768x432.jpeg 768w" data-srcset-webp="https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1.jpeg.webp 932w, https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1-300x169.jpeg.webp 300w, https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1-150x84.jpeg.webp 150w, https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1-768x432.jpeg.webp 768w" height="338" loading="lazy" src="https://www.darkmoon.me/wp-content/ewww/lazy/placeholder-601x338.png" width="601"/><noscript><img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-153839" data-eio="l" height="338" sizes="(max-width: 601px) 100vw, 601px" src="https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1.jpeg" srcset="https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1.jpeg 932w, https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1-300x169.jpeg 300w, https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1-150x84.jpeg 150w, https://www.darkmoon.me/uploads/Pelosi-Biden-16x9-1-768x432.jpeg 768w" width="601"/></noscript></p>
