</div>
<div class="entrybody">
<p><a href="http://farmwars.info/?p=18653"><strong><em>Farm Wars</em></strong></a></p>
<figure class="wp-block-image is-resized"><img alt="" class="wp-image-18665" height="323" src="http://farmwars.info/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/crime-against-humanity.jpg" width="580"/></figure>
<h4>BY <a href="https://greatreject.org/author/greatreject/">GREATREJECT</a> · 24/02/2021</h4>
<h3>A second Nuremberg Tribunal has been prepared since last week and a class action is being set up</h3>
<h5>A class action, or representative action, is a lawsuit in which a large group of people collectively bring a claim to court, or in which a certain class of defendants is sued</h5>
<p><strong><em><a href="https://www.fuellmich.com/dr-reiner-fuellmich/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Reiner Fuellmich</a></em></strong>  is getting that support from the thousands of lawyers worldwide to bring the Covid19 scandal set up in <em><strong><a href="https://reseauinternational.net/le-plus-grand-nuremberg-de-tous-les-temps-se-prepare/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Davos</a></strong></em> to the <em><strong><a href="https://uncutnews.ch/hochexplosives-aus-frankreich-vorbereitung-eines-zweites-nuernberger-tribunal-unter-der-schirmherrschaft-tausender-globaler-anwaelte-hinter-dem-anwalt-reiner-fuellmich/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">tribunal</a></strong></em>.</p>
<p>Reiner Fuellmich is the lawyer who succeeded in co-convicting the car giant Volkswagen because of its modified catalytic converters.</p>
<p>And it is the same lawyer who succeeded in <em><strong><a href="https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/deutsche-bank-agrees-pay-over-130-million-resolve-foreign-corrupt-practices-act-and-fraud" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">condemning</a></strong> <strong><a href="https://www.dgap.de/dgap/News/press_release/bundesverband-anleger-und-verbraucherrecht-juristische-praxis-sieht-der-musterfeststellungsklage-keine-loesung-der-bestehenden-rechtsungleichheit/?newsID=1073839" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Deutsche Bank</a></strong></em> as a <a href="https://msochartered.com/2020/10/08/covid-intentional-tort-class-action-suits/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><strong><em>criminal</em></strong></a> <a href="https://taz.de/Betrugsvorwurf-gegen-die-Deutsche-Bank/!5389570/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><em><strong>enterprise</strong></em></a>.</p>
<p>Deutsche Bank agreed to pay more than $130 million to resolve/buy off the case because of foreign corruption practices and <em><strong><a href="https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/deutsche-bank-agrees-pay-over-130-million-resolve-foreign-corrupt-practices-act-and-fraud" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">fraud cases</a></strong></em>.</p>
<h3>Covid-19 becomes fraud case of the century</h3>
<p>So now he faces his next job. The job of a lifetime, supported by thousands of lawyers and experts worldwide.</p>
<p><strong>According to Reiner Fuellmich, all the frauds committed by German companies are nothing compared to the damage this Covid-19 scam has caused and continues to cause.</strong></p>
<p>This Covid-19 crisis should be renamed the <strong>Covid-19 scandal</strong> and all those responsible should be prosecuted for civil damages resulting from manipulation and falsified testing protocols.</p>
<p>Therefore, an international network of corporate lawyers will litigate the <strong>biggest tort case of all time</strong> namely The Covid-19 fraud scandal which has since become the <strong>biggest crime against humanity</strong> ever.</p>
<p>On the initiative of a group of German lawyers, a Covid-19 commission of inquiry was set up with the aim of bringing collective action on an international level on the <em><strong><a href="https://reseauinternational.net/le-plus-grand-nuremberg-de-tous-les-temps-se-prepare/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">basis of</a></strong></em> Anglo-Saxon <em><strong><a href="https://rgnpress.ro/2021/02/22/se-pregateste-al-doilea-tribunal-nuremberg-cel-mai-mare-proces-din-toate-timpurile-cine-va-fi-inculpat-din-romania-arafat-rafila-cercel/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">law</a></strong></em>.</p>
<h3>Covid-19 scandal never been a health issue</h3>
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote"><p>The hearings of, among others, hundreds of scientists, doctors, economists and lawyers of international repute conducted since October 7, 2020 by the Berlin Commission of Inquiry into the Covid-19 affair have now demonstrated with a probability approaching 100% certainty that the Covid-19 scandal was never a health issue  <em>Dr. Fuëlmich on February 15, 2020</em></p></blockquote>
<p>Court sessions can be found <a href="https://corona-ausschuss.de/sitzungen/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank"><em><strong>here</strong></em></a></p>
<p><strong>Rather, it was a matter of strengthening power, illegally obtained by criminal methods of the corrupt Davos clique, by transferring the wealth of the people to the members of Davos on the backs of small, and especially medium-sized businesses, among others.</strong></p>
<p>The committees conclusion is that Covid-19 is being used as a diversionary tactic by business and political elites with the goal of shifting market shares and wealth from small and medium-sized businesses to global platforms such as Amazon, Google, Uber, and other big tech and multinational <em><strong><a href="https://nouveau-monde.ca/fr/le-plus-grand-nuremberg-de-tous-les-temps-se-prepare/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">corporations</a></strong></em>.</p>

<p>Appeal to annul the approval of a vaccination filed against the European Commission, lawsuit in New York on the status of PCR testing, German lawsuits, Canadian lawsuits, Australian lawsuits, Austrian lawsuits, lawsuits at the International Court of Justice and at the European Court of Human Rights.</p>

<p>To make matters worse, the health and economic damage caused by the anticovid measures is so devastating that it is necessary to speak of a<strong> historically unique level of destruction</strong>.</p>

<h2><a href="http://farmwars.info/?p=16203" rel="bookmark" title="Link to Truth  A Casualty of Expedience">Truth  A Casualty of Expedience</a></h2>
</div>
<div class="endate">Barbara H. Peterson on February 3rd, 2021</div>
</div>
<div class="entrybody">
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter is-resized"><img alt="" class="wp-image-16206" height="474" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" src="http://farmwars.info/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/truth-casualty-of-war.jpg" srcset="http://farmwars.info/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/truth-casualty-of-war.jpg 1000w, http://farmwars.info/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/truth-casualty-of-war-300x284.jpg 300w, http://farmwars.info/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/truth-casualty-of-war-768x728.jpg 768w" width="500"/></figure></div>
<p><a href="http://www.mausproductions.com/"><strong>D Peter Maus</strong></a></p>
<p><strong><em><a href="http://farmwars.info/?p=16203">Farm Wars</a></em></strong></p>
<p>There was a moment, in the TV series, Shark, with James
Woods, when his character walked into a room of his associate attorneys, and he
said, The truth is relative. Pick one that works. That was a wake
up call. Because it expressed a truth wed been living since the evolution of
Bernays work evolved from selling soap to government distractions. </p>
<p>The deeper we get into this government reset, driven by
China, and Soros, and accomplished through manipulation of the masses starving
for something to be passionate about, the more obvious it becomes that the
Truth has become a casualty of expedience. </p>
<p>For decades, we suspended all good sense, ethics, our
passions, even values and some laws in the name of making a buck. Now, dont
get me wrong, Im a good capitalist, and a thriving economy is a good thing. A
very good thing. But, at the loss of who we are? Not so much. I have friends
across the pond, who live a very different life. They work, and enjoy their
fruits, but they also value time used to enjoy them, along with family,
friends, a scenic view, and a glass of wine, or the moment to enjoy the smell a
good cup of coffee. We look at them as slackers, as we go-go-go for work,
earning a living, having things, and getting a $5 cup o joe in the drive thru,
but somehow missing that aroma, by not sitting down with the newspaper, or a
good conversation, before stepping into the fray. Its a criminal loss of life
to not take the moment to savor it. </p>
<p>I just got into a pissing match with a guy in a high end
audio group, who was decrying an audio advertisement for the use of a pretty
girl dancing to the music of a high end system. He called us all dinosaurs. I
pointed out that if were all to be extinct, why would we not wish to enjoy a
good tune and a pretty girl along the way. In this faux silver plated, digital,
compact, living-through-an-iPhone existence, a little passion is a good thing.
And, taking a moment to appreciate the image of a pretty girl expressing her
passions, in a room full of high end gear, is no different than taking that
moment of sitting on a porch overlooking the Amalfi coast with a fresh cup of
coffee. </p>
<p>If youre not living, whats the point? And, that may be the
one of the two truths that weve lost sight of, in our rush to begin rushing:
That to live without enjoyment, simple as it may be, is the closest thing to
hell were going to experience, here, on Earth. And, that to experience
enjoyment, you really do have to be truthful to who and what you are. And, that
truth extends to the body of humanity, regardless of where they may be. </p>
<p>To live the way we live, in this country, especially, today,
is to live in a state of packing lies upon lies to justify being, and staying,
this unhappy. And, when the lies are exposed, even trivial ones, explosions of
horror result. If we look closely at what led up to it, we see that the
execution of LaVoy Finicum was no more than the silencing of a man whod chosen
to expose a single layer of the lies he was living under. Silenced by the very
government he helped put into office based on the lies governments have been
telling since Bernays made lying a science morphed into an art form. </p>
<p>Can you blame people for being offended at the disrespect shown by someone who tells the truth, in a culture based on lies?</p>
<p>D Peter Maus</p>

<p></p>
<p>Ever since the pantomime plague began, your local friendly crazy quackaloons have been telling you the diagnostic test isnt fit for purpose and creates false positives, due to results being amplified too many times.</p>
<p>Now the WHO states the diagnostic test isnt fit for purpose and creates false positives, due to results being amplified too many times.</p>
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter"><img alt="" class="wp-image-16192" sizes="(max-width: 491px) 100vw, 491px" src="http://farmwars.info/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/image-1.png" srcset="http://farmwars.info/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/image-1.png 491w, http://farmwars.info/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/image-1-300x262.png 300w"/></figure></div>
<p>For the hard of thinking and BBC-watchers (but I repeat myself
), I will explain
again what the PCR test does: it detects genetic sequences of viruses, but NOT
viruses themselves. The more amplification cycles a test result goes through,
the more genetic sequences it detects. As we all have all sorts of genetic
debris floating about inside of us, to quote the inventor of the PCR test,
anyone can test positive for anything with the PCR. It doesnt tell you
youre sick.</p>
<p>Read this salutary tale from 2007 for what happens when PCR tests are inappropriately used to diagnose infectious illness:</p>
<p></p>
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter"><img alt="" class="wp-image-16190" sizes="(max-width: 501px) 100vw, 501px" src="http://farmwars.info/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/image.png" srcset="http://farmwars.info/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/image.png 501w, http://farmwars.info/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/image-300x201.png 300w"/></figure></div>
<p style="text-align:center"><a href="https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.nytimes.com%2F2007%2F01%2F22%2Fhealth%2F22whoop.html%3Ffbclid%3DIwAR1TDkgiUjgZw0d1TRcI2-pvOc75EnUXk1hCjGnR-oUQufHfhLehb5K8Myo&amp;h=AT30bShko0I0QOoP9ya_CMynWMbAbOO8uXdbA4S85l-FjbRk0A8Enef9gY_zYAE-gp_-8iSQQiKWdpUWf2cYdajyVNtPfMGvxHTP4SJZOLCY2CT0mWHarnLslkY3h3sGHk8&amp;__tn__=-UK-R&amp;c%5b0%5d=AT06rGu7dDFZjxKLDcGRnLfwmXlIpv-gIvZZJ_1bOTxVNpHiH6K3VgUZfY8YR9X1Rm03hKJPE_IDEb9-iZlAUvZ_JLgHa8SWQoI615PtMl2Chai6xshKZkE48XbzqsGSO6hAexDVOlz3A5OAFkdmUGmodw" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">https://www.nytimes.com/2007/01/22/health/22whoop.html</a></p>
<p>So, if you happen to be in the midst of orchestrating a
pretend plague and want to create a lot of positive cases to
terrify the masses, you simply amplify the PCR test results a lot of times.
Have you noticed how the propaganda press is always going on about rises in
CASES? Not deaths. Not hospitalisations. Not even the sniffles. Just
cases. A case is a positive PCR result, and hence, in
reality, meaningless. It doesnt tell us anything about whether more people are
ill or not.</p>
<p>Nevertheless, most people take headlines at face value and
dont and cant think (critical thinking having been intentionally axed from
the national curriculum years ago), so they believe an explosion in
cases a pandemic makes.</p>
<p>Oh, but you know someone who was really sick with
Covid? Im heartless for denying its severity? Look: I dont deny you
knew someone very sick. They may even have died. But elderly and unwell people,
and sometimes younger people, do die. Its tragic, but its life. In normal
circumstances, 1,600 people die every day in the UK alone. That number hasnt
significantly increased. Just because they tested positive on an
unfit-for-purpose test shortly before their death does not prove they died
from Covid, and it certainly doesnt prove theres a pandemic.</p>
<p>The PCR test has been used to create the illusion of a
pandemic to manufacture your consent for a variety of despotically draconian
and ultra-authoritarian measures that you never would have accepted unless you
had been scare-mongered into compliance. The best way to control people is to
scare them  all repressive regimes know that.</p>
<p>But  now we have the miraculous saviour vaccine, so the
propaganda angle has got to change.</p>
<p>Hence  the WHO is now telling labs across the world to
reduce the number of PCR cycles. This means a dramatic decrease in positive
test results, which will be used as evidence the so-called vaccine
is working.</p>
<p>Meanwhile, as bad reactions to the experimental <a href="http://farmwars.info/?p=16181">gene therapies</a> being marketed as vaccines mount up, this will be used as evidence for why we need to keep the restrictions, as bad vaccine reactions will be blamed on the virus mutating and as evidence we dont have herd immunity. We will be told new and different vaccines are needed every six months or so to fight the never-ending new strains.</p>
<p>So, both restrictions and vaccines will go on and on and on.
This will last until at least 2025, by which point, you will have been fully
and irreversibly re-engineered, to be terrified of other people, to eschew all
physical contact, to smother your airways and hide your face, and to view your
fellow human beings with deep suspicion and hostility, snitching to the
authorities if they break the rules, and viewing them as nothing
but filthy disease vectors and potential mass murderers. Thats if you survive
the next five years, of course, which, if you keep getting vaccinated, is not
necessarily very likely.  especially if youre older and/or have existing
health problems.</p>
<p>Thats the future in store for you if you keep believing the
execrable and mendacious lies being fed to you by your felonious government,
and their state propaganda organs the press.</p>
<p>Yes, I know Im just a crazy conspiracy theorist, and why should anyone listen to me? But you know what the biggest conspiracy theory of all time is? Trusting the Government, and believing they have your best interests at heart.</p>
