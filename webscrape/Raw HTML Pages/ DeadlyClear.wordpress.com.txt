<div class="entry-content">
<p class="has-medium-font-size">Regarding the 2020 Foreclosure &amp; Eviction Moratorium</p>
<p>By Sydney Sullivan</p>
<p class="has-drop-cap has-normal-font-size">For nearly a dozen years, we have followed the securitization/rehypothecation crisis and corruption on behalf of over 200 million American Homeowners (2.5 per HH) and their families. It’s been a tough 12 years and some families are still fighting fraudulent bank foreclosures since 2009. Can you even imagine a lawsuit and the threat of losing your home for over a decade, waking up every morning wondering when the sheriff is coming to throw you, your family and your belongings out on the street?</p>
<p class="has-normal-font-size"></p>
<div class="wp-block-media-text alignwide is-stacked-on-mobile"><figure class="wp-block-media-text__media"><img alt="" class="wp-image-9598" data-attachment-id="9598" data-comments-opened="1" data-image-description="" data-image-meta='{"aperture":"0","credit":"","camera":"","caption":"","created_timestamp":"0","copyright":"","focal_length":"0","iso":"0","shutter_speed":"0","title":"","orientation":"0"}' data-image-title="banker_debt_web" data-large-file="https://deadlyclear.files.wordpress.com/2020/09/banker_debt_web.jpg?w=584" data-medium-file="https://deadlyclear.files.wordpress.com/2020/09/banker_debt_web.jpg?w=300" data-orig-file="https://deadlyclear.files.wordpress.com/2020/09/banker_debt_web.jpg" data-orig-size="800,584" data-permalink="https://deadlyclear.com/banker_debt_web-2/" sizes="(max-width: 800px) 100vw, 800px" src="https://deadlyclear.files.wordpress.com/2020/09/banker_debt_web.jpg?w=800" srcset="https://deadlyclear.files.wordpress.com/2020/09/banker_debt_web.jpg 800w, https://deadlyclear.files.wordpress.com/2020/09/banker_debt_web.jpg?w=150 150w, https://deadlyclear.files.wordpress.com/2020/09/banker_debt_web.jpg?w=300 300w, https://deadlyclear.files.wordpress.com/2020/09/banker_debt_web.jpg?w=768 768w"/></figure><div class="wp-block-media-text__content">
<p class="has-normal-font-size">Thank you in advance for considering our plight. We applaud your “Moratorium” on evictions and foreclosures and the extension which you added just this week – but Sirs, it’s very limited. It doesn’t reach a lot of people that are in dire need and let us tell you why.</p>

<span class="sep">Posted on </span><a href="https://deadlyclear.com/2020/07/07/held-hostage-in-politically-biased-disingenuoustwitterprison-by-twitter-extortion/" rel="bookmark" title="8:48 pm"><time class="entry-date" datetime="2020-07-07T20:48:37-10:00">July 7, 2020</time></a><span class="by-author"> <span class="sep"> by </span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="https://deadlyclear.com/author/deadlyclear/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Deadly Clear">Deadly Clear</a></span></span> </div><!-- .entry-meta -->

<div class="entry-content">
<p>By Sydney Sullivan</p>
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="alignleft is-resized"><img alt="*freedom of speech *social media *shoot to kill *protests ..." height="192" src="https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=http%3A%2F%2F99getsmart.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F10%2Ftwitter_jail.jpg&amp;f=1&amp;nofb=1" width="256"/><figcaption>Disingenuous Twitter Prison – Limited Freedom of Speech &amp; Due Process</figcaption></figure></div>
<p>If Twitter wasn’t so juvenile and painfully politically biased this Twitter prison sentence wouldn’t be worth my time to post. But since COVID-19 has turned into a political weapon rather than a imminent concern, its very hard to take any politician seriously on the subject.</p>
<p>As litigation paralegals, we research everything and weigh the evidence. There is more information available that confirms face masks are more unhealthy than any good they actually do. In the early phases of this COVID scare, we made over 200 masks for our local community. We were careful to use recommended material and even added an iron-on interfacing with more secure strapping. </p>
<p>But as time went on, people in the stores had paper masks hanging off their faces and more stories about how useless masks were against such a tiny virus began to surface. Dr. Fauci didn’t help either. One day he was “no masks needed” and the next time “wear masks”. And let’s face it, politicians are not always the brightest light bulbs on the planet – so whatever they hear last is where they stand.</p>
